GongConnect - Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how GongConnect collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

GongConnect does not collect or store personal information from its users. Any data used within the application is handled securely and solely for its intended functionality.

3. How We Use Information

GongConnect interacts with TikTok’s API to perform tasks such as video uploads. No user data is stored or shared outside of the application’s intended use.

4. Third-Party Services

GongConnect relies on TikTok’s API to provide its core functionality. Users are subject to TikTok’s Privacy Policy when interacting with their services through GongConnect.

5. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. GongConnect uses secure methods to interact with TikTok’s API and does not store sensitive information on external servers.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of GongConnect after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
